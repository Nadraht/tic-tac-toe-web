Tic Tac Toe

Rules

Tic Tac Toe is a simple yet strategic game where you and the computer take turns marking spaces in a 3×3 grid.

🎯 Objective

The goal is to place three of your marks "X" in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row before your opponent (the computer) does.

🕹️ How to Play

  • Click on any empty square to make your move.
  • The computer will automatically make its move after yours.
  • The game ends when either player wins or when the board is full (a draw).

🏆 Difficulty Levels

  • Easy: The computer makes random moves.
  • Normal: The computer uses basic strategy to block or win.
  • Hard: The computer uses an advanced algorithm for optimal play.

📊 Scoring

  • Player win → +1 point to Player
  • Computer win → +1 point to Computer
  • Draw → +1 point to Draws

Try to beat the computer on all levels and track your progress on the Scores page!
